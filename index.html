Our Story

In 2016, two roommates Derek and Toby found that cannabis could help many of their chronic health issues. They expected their friends and family to be happy but were surprised to find many people didn't accept their newfound community. That's why Derek and Toby created WeedFan.net as a way to push back against cannabis stigma and bring more people into the community that has helped so many people.

As experienced software engineers: Derek and Toby take web technologies seriously. They strive to make WeedFan.net not just the best 420-friendly web hosting provider, but the best web hosting provider, period. Read more about our technology below.
